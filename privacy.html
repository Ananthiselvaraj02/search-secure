Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. Read below to understand how we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

At Secure Search, we prioritize your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) while you use our service. When using our search engine, your searches remain anonymous.

2. Search Queries

We do not store or track your search queries. All search data is processed temporarily to provide results but is discarded immediately after. Your searches are never tied back to you personally.

3. Cookies

Secure Search does not use cookies to track your activity or store your personal data. Some third-party services may use cookies; however, we do not control these services. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our search results may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of those websites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

5. Data Security

Secure Search is designed to be secure. We use encryption and other security measures to protect your data. Since we do not store personal data, the risk of exposure is minimal.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revision date. Your continued use of Secure Search implies your acceptance of the updated terms.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at support@securesearch.com.

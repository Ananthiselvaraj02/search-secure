About Secure Search

Your go-to platform for secure and private searches.

Our Mission

At Secure Search, our mission is to empower users with a safe, secure, and private online search experience. We understand the importance of privacy in today’s digital age, where personal data can be easily tracked and shared. That’s why Secure Search is designed to protect your search history and prevent your data from being stored or accessed by third parties. By using Search Encrypt, your searches are encrypted, providing an additional layer of security.

Our Values

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of privacy and security in everything we do. Our core values are:

  • Privacy First: We ensure that your search data remains your own. We do not track or store any information.
  • Security: Your searches are encrypted, protecting your data from malicious activities or unauthorized access.
  • User Control: You are in full control of your online experience. No advertisements, no profiling, no personalized tracking.
  • Transparency: We operate with complete transparency. You know what we do, and more importantly, what we don’t do—track, log, or store your search data.

Our Story

Founded in response to growing concerns about online privacy, Secure Search was created to give users a better option for internet searches. As privacy advocates, we saw the need for a search engine that didn’t sacrifice user safety for convenience. Using state-of-the-art technology, we built Secure Search to ensure your data is never tracked or stored. We partnered with Search Encrypt, one of the most trusted privacy-first search engines, to offer a fully secure and encrypted search experience. Our goal is simple: give users the ability to search online with confidence, knowing their personal data is protected at all times.
